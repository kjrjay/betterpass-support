BetterPass Support

Help for setup, Safari AutoFill, iCloud availability, and account recovery.

Contact Support

If you need help with BetterPass, contact: betterpass.help@gmail.com

Requirements

  • BetterPass is a macOS app.
  • You must be signed in to iCloud on your Mac.
  • If iCloud is unavailable, unlock is blocked by design.
  • Safari AutoFill must be enabled in System Settings to use autofill.

How to Enable Safari AutoFill

  • Open System Settings on your Mac.
  • Go to General → Passwords → Password Options.
  • Enable BetterPass as an AutoFill provider.
  • Open Safari and sign in to a site to test autofill.

Troubleshooting

  • BetterPass says iCloud is unavailable: confirm you are signed in to iCloud and that iCloud services are currently available on your Mac.
  • AutoFill is not appearing in Safari: verify that BetterPass AutoFill is enabled in System Settings and try quitting and reopening Safari.
  • Touch ID unlock is unavailable: make sure Touch ID is enabled on your Mac and that BetterPass unlock material was previously saved on this device.
  • Forgot Password is unavailable: recovery works only if Touch ID unlock material was previously saved on that same Mac.
  • You are locked out: if recovery is unavailable, the remaining option may be account reset, which deletes vault data.
