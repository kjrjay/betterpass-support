# BetterPass Privacy Policy Effective date: March 26, 2026 BetterPass is a macOS password manager. This Privacy Policy explains how BetterPass handles information when you use the app. ## Information BetterPass Handles BetterPass may handle information that you choose to store in the app, including: - Password entry names - Usernames - Passwords - Website URLs - Notes - Folder or organizational metadata BetterPass also handles security-related data needed to operate the app, including: - Master password verification data - Encrypted vault key material - Optional Touch ID unlock material stored locally on your Mac when you choose to enable it If you contact support, we may receive the information you choose to include in your email. ## How BetterPass Uses Information BetterPass uses information only to provide the app’s functionality, including to: - Store and organize your saved credentials - Encrypt and decrypt your vault data - Unlock the app using your master password - Enable optional Touch ID unlock on supported Macs - Provide Safari AutoFill functionality - Support account recovery features that depend on locally saved unlock material ## Storage and Sync BetterPass is designed to use Apple-provided storage and sync services associated with your Apple ID. Depending on the feature, your data may be stored in: - Your local Mac keychain - iCloud Keychain - Your private CloudKit database Optional Touch ID unlock material is stored locally on the Mac where you enable it and is not intended to sync to other devices. BetterPass is designed so that iCloud availability is required for normal setup and unlock flows. ## Data Sharing BetterPass does not sell your personal information. BetterPass does not use third-party advertising SDKs. BetterPass does not use third-party analytics or cross-app tracking SDKs. BetterPass does not share your data with data brokers. Apple may process data as needed to provide services such as iCloud, CloudKit, Keychain, and Safari AutoFill. Those services are governed by Apple’s own terms and privacy policies. ## Tracking BetterPass does not track you across apps, websites, or services owned by other companies. ## Data Retention and Deletion Your data remains stored until you delete it, remove local app data, or use account reset features provided by the app. You can delete individual vault entries from within BetterPass. You can also remove locally stored Touch ID unlock material from within the app’s settings on that Mac. If you use the app’s account reset flow, vault data may be deleted from iCloud and from the local device as part of that process. ## Security BetterPass is designed to use encryption, keychain protection, and Apple platform security features to protect stored credentials and unlock material. No method of electronic storage or transmission is guaranteed to be completely secure, but BetterPass is designed to minimize unnecessary data exposure and to keep sensitive data protected using Apple platform services. ## Children’s Privacy BetterPass is not directed to children under 13. ## Changes to This Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.
